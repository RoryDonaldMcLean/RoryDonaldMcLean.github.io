> About the Project
Created in Unity Game Engine.
Coded in c#
University Project: Undertaken during Masters Course.

Androids in a Lonely World is a cooperative local multiplayer puzzle game which was developed as part of my final term at Abertay University's Masters course. The brief of the project which was given by Arron Simpson- Lead artist at Riot Games. The breif was a cooperative multiplayer game with a narrative that would help form an emotional connection between the two players. This was done by creating a simple puzzle game where both players control a pair of robots which have to exchange parts in order to navigate the enviroment and pick up and move puzzle elements. As the game progressed the Robots would slowly begin to fall apart forcing the players to cooperate and exchange more of their parts in order to progress.
