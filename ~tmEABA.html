About Me

Hello! I am Rory Donald McLean and I am currently a Student in Professional Masters in Game Development at Abertay University.

For as long as I can remember I’ve been playing video games and wanted nothing more than to create my own someday. Video games are my passion, I play them, I make them, I read about them and I study them.

I was inspired to join the games industry largely due to my industry hero Hideo Kojima, I want to inspire the next generation of gamer's to create their own games and be part of the industry I love. When I’m not playing or making games I’m either at home playing them or learning how to spar with a German Longsword at my local HEMA (Historical European Martial Arts) group RMAS (Renaissance Martial Arts Society). I have taken part in a number of Game Jams over the years such as Global Game Jam where I’ve worked as both a programmer and game designer alongside some of my friends and fellow students to create some amazing small games, one of which can be found below.

I’ve also worked on a number of professional projects working as both a programmer and team lead. These projects include creating a Cyber-Security learning game for NCR and a 2D dungeon game for BAFTA, both of which were part of my course at Abertay University and working with a small indie team which we presented to DARE in 2016.
