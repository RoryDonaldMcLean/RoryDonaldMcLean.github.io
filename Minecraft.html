> About the Project
Both experiencing the rise of Minecraft and having small nephews that adore the game, along with being a Dundee resident in close proximity to the fantastic 4J Studios, I thought I'd try my hand at a small "Design" like project using Minecraft itself.

Being a huge fan of Capcom's survival Horror Series, Resident Evil. I decided to recreate the entire Spencer Mansion from Resident Evil: Remake. By both playing through the game again and taking note of each rooms details, decorations and placement in the world, I managed to recreate every room from each part of the map, from the chilling halls of the mansion to the zombie filled secret laboratory below.
