About Me

Hello! I am Rory Donald McLean and I am currently a Student in Professional Masters in Game Development at Abertay University. I have taken part in a number of Game Jams over the years such as Global Game Jam where I've worked as both a programmer and game designer alongside some of my friends and fellow students to create some amazing small games, one of which can be found above. I've also worked on a number of professional projects working as both a programmer and team lead. These projects include creating a Cyber-Security learning game for NCR and a 2D dungeon game for BAFTA, both of which were part of my course at Abertay University and working with a small indie team which we presented to DARE in 2016.
